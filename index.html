Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

What is a Freedom Number?

+

Your Freedom Number is the monthly revenue your side hustle needs to generate to safely replace your current job income. It equals your monthly expenses — because that's the only number that matters for your lifestyle. Supporting targets include a 6-month savings runway and an optional 75% salary cushion for comfort.

How does Atlas help me?

+

Atlas is your AI freedom agent. It analyzes your profile (skills, hours, finances, interests) and recommends side hustles matched to you. Then it breaks your goals into executable missions, drafts content, tracks progress, detects burnout, and alerts you when you're quit-ready. Think of it as a business advisor + accountability partner + motivational coach, all in one.

Can I use LADO while working full-time?

+

Absolutely. That's the whole point. LADO is designed specifically for people with 9-to-5 jobs who want to build something on the side. Atlas helps you optimize your available hours (early mornings, evenings, weekends) and creates a schedule that doesn't burn you out. Most users launch their first side hustle while keeping their day job.

What if I don't know what side hustle to start?

+

That's exactly what the Discover engine does. Share your skills, available hours, interests, and budget constraints. Atlas will surface 3-5 side hustle ideas ranked by time-to-first-dollar, startup cost, and market opportunity. Each recommendation includes a launch checklist, so you know exactly what to do next.

Is the $10 deposit refundable?

+

The $10 deposit is a one-time payment that locks in founding member pricing. It's applied directly to your first month's subscription, so your actual cost is $19 for Starter, $39 for Pro, or $89 for Unlimited in month one. We offer a 7-day full refund policy if LADO isn't right for you — refunds apply to the deposit only if requested within 7 days of purchase.
