Refund Policy

1. Overview

At Costa Spirits LLC, we want you to be satisfied with LADO. This refund policy outlines when and how you can request refunds for your subscription or early access deposit. Please read this policy carefully before making a purchase.

2. Subscription Refunds

2.1 Monthly Subscription Refund Policy

LADO operates on a monthly subscription model. We do not offer refunds for partial months of service. However, you retain full control over your subscription and can cancel at any time.

2.2 How Cancellation Works

When you cancel your subscription:

  • Your cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period (not immediately)
  • You will not be charged for the following month
  • You retain full access to LADO until the end of your billing period
  • No refund is issued for the month you already paid for (you paid for a full month of access, which you retain through the end of that month)

2.3 Example

Example: If you subscribe on March 1 and pay $39 for March access, you have full access all month. If you cancel on March 15, you keep your access through March 31 at no extra cost. You will not be charged on April 1, and you will not receive a refund for the March payment.

2.4 Canceling Your Subscription

You can cancel your subscription anytime from your account settings on LADO. No explanation required. Cancellation is immediate in the system, and you will not be charged for the next billing cycle.

2.5 Exceptional Cases

If you experience a significant technical issue that prevents you from using LADO (e.g., account lockout, platform outage lasting more than 24 hours), please contact us at tommy@costaspirits.com. We will evaluate your case and may offer a credit or refund at our discretion.

3. Early Access Deposit Refunds

3.1 Deposit Refund Eligibility

The $10 early access deposit is fully refundable within 30 days of payment, provided you have NOT activated a paid subscription.

3.2 Timeline

  • Day 0-30: Deposit is fully refundable if no paid subscription is activated
  • Day 31 onwards: Deposit is forfeited if no paid subscription has been activated

3.3 What Counts as "Activation"

A paid subscription is considered activated when:

  • You upgrade to a paid plan from your early access account
  • Your first billing charge on a paid plan is processed

3.4 Deposits and Paid Subscriptions

If you activate a paid subscription, your $10 deposit is automatically applied as a credit toward your first month's subscription fee. Once a paid subscription is activated, the deposit is non-refundable.

3.5 Deposit Reminder

We will send you a reminder email at day 25 of your 30-day window reminding you to activate a paid subscription or request a refund. If you do not take action by day 30, your deposit will be forfeited.

4. How to Request a Refund

Step 1: Email Your Request

Send an email to tommy@costaspirits.com with the subject line:

Refund Request - [Your Name]

Include the following information in your email:

  • Your full name
  • Email address associated with your LADO account
  • Reason for requesting a refund (optional but helpful)
  • Date you made the payment

Step 2: We Review Your Request

We will review your request within 2 business days and confirm that you are eligible for a refund.

Step 3: Refund Processing

If approved, your refund will be processed to the original payment method (the card you used for the transaction). Processing typically takes 5-10 business days, but may vary depending on your bank.

Step 4: Confirmation

You will receive a confirmation email once the refund has been processed. Check your bank or credit card statement to verify the refund appears.

5. Refund Exceptions

We cannot issue refunds in the following cases:

5.1 Subscription Payments

  • Monthly subscriptions are non-refundable for the month already paid (though you can cancel before the next billing cycle)
  • Refunds for "lost productivity" or "not getting results" are not provided, as stated in our Terms of Service

5.2 Deposits

  • Deposits forfeited after 30 days without activation of a paid subscription
  • Deposits already applied to a paid subscription are non-refundable

5.3 Chargebacks

If you initiate a chargeback through your bank or credit card company without first contacting us, we may dispute the chargeback and take appropriate action, which could include terminating your account and reporting the activity to relevant authorities.

6. Refund Processing Time

Once your refund is approved and processed, it typically appears in your original payment method within 5-10 business days. In some cases, your bank may take additional time to credit your account.

If your refund does not appear within 10 business days, contact us immediately at tommy@costaspirits.com with proof of your refund request.

7. Account Suspension After Refund

If we process a refund for a deposit and you haven't activated a paid subscription, your early access account will remain active for the remainder of your access period. After a refund of a subscription payment (in exceptional cases), your account access will be terminated.
