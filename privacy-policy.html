Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Costa Spirits LLC ("we," "us," "Company") operates LADO (www.lado.club), a platform that helps users plan and execute side hustles to achieve financial freedom. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information from you in the following ways:

2.1 Account Information

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Job title, skills, and professional background
  • Password (hashed and encrypted)
  • Profile preferences and customizations

2.2 Financial Information

  • Monthly income and expenses (used to calculate your Freedom Number)
  • Current savings and side hustle revenue
  • Payment information (credit card details are handled securely by Stripe and not stored directly on our servers)

2.3 Usage Data

  • Pages visited and features used
  • Time spent on the platform
  • Content you create (missions, business plans, product descriptions)
  • Interactions with Atlas (our AI agent)

2.4 Device Information

  • IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Device type and unique identifiers
  • Approximate location (city/state level)

2.5 Communications

  • Messages and conversations with Atlas
  • Support emails and feedback you submit

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

3.1 To Provide the Service

  • Create and manage your account
  • Deliver Atlas recommendations and guidance
  • Process payments and subscriptions
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests

3.2 To Personalize Your Experience

  • Generate side hustle ideas matched to your skills and available time
  • Calculate your Freedom Number and quit-ready score
  • Provide tailored AI recommendations based on your profile

3.3 To Improve the Platform

  • Analyze usage patterns and feature engagement
  • Identify technical issues and bugs
  • Develop new features and improvements

3.4 For Marketing and Communications

  • Send service announcements and updates
  • Send promotional emails (only if you opt in)

3.5 For Legal and Security

  • Comply with laws and regulations
  • Protect against fraud and security threats
  • Enforce our Terms of Service

4. AI and Data Processing

LADO uses Claude, an AI model provided by Anthropic, to generate personalized recommendations and guidance. Here's how your data is processed:

4.1 Data Sharing with Anthropic

When you interact with Atlas or request AI-generated recommendations, your profile information and messages are transmitted to Anthropic's servers to process the AI response. This includes:

  • Your financial metrics (income, expenses, savings)
  • Your skills, available hours, and side hustle details
  • Conversations with Atlas

4.2 AI Model Training

Your data is NOT used to train Anthropic's AI models. Anthropic does not use data from LADO to improve or train Claude. Your information remains separate and is only used to provide you with personalized recommendations.

4.3 Data Retention

Anthropic retains conversation data for up to 30 days for safety and monitoring purposes, then deletes it. You can request that your data be deleted from Anthropic's servers at any time by contacting us.

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We only share your information with trusted service providers who help us deliver LADO:

5.1 Payment Processing

Stripe processes all payments and subscriptions. We share only the information necessary to process your payment (name, email, subscription plan). Stripe's privacy policy governs their handling of your payment data.

5.2 Data Storage

Supabase hosts our database and stores your account information, financial data, and content. Supabase is compliant with industry security standards and data protection regulations.

5.3 AI Processing

Anthropic processes your data through Claude when you interact with Atlas. See section 4 above for details on how your data is handled.

5.4 Other Disclosures

We may disclose your information if:

  • Required by law or legal process
  • Necessary to protect the rights or safety of LADO or our users
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (you would be notified of any such change)

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

6.1 Encryption

  • All data in transit is encrypted using SSL/TLS (HTTPS)
  • Sensitive data at rest is encrypted in our database

6.2 Access Controls

  • Only authorized team members have access to personal data
  • Access is logged and monitored

6.3 Password Security

  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • We recommend using a strong, unique password

6.4 Limitations

While we take security seriously, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data. If you have security concerns, contact us immediately.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you have certain rights regarding your personal data:

7.1 Access

You have the right to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you. Contact us at tommy@costaspirits.com with the subject "Data Access Request."

7.2 Correction

You can update or correct your account information directly in your LADO profile or by contacting us.

7.3 Deletion

You have the right to request that we delete your personal data (subject to certain legal exceptions). We will delete your account and associated data within 30 days of your request.

7.4 Data Portability

You can request your data in a standard format (CSV, JSON) so you can transfer it to another service.

7.5 Opt-Out of Marketing

You can unsubscribe from promotional emails at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in the email or by contacting us.

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal tracking on LADO:

8.1 Essential Cookies

We use cookies to maintain your session and keep you logged in. These are necessary for the platform to function.

8.2 Analytics

We use basic analytics to understand how users interact with LADO (pages visited, time on site). This helps us improve the platform. You can opt out of analytics by disabling cookies in your browser settings.

8.3 No Third-Party Advertising

We do not use third-party advertising networks or sell your data to advertisers.

9. Children's Privacy

LADO is not intended for users under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If we become aware that a minor has provided us with personal data, we will delete such information promptly. If you believe we have collected data from a minor, contact us immediately at tommy@costaspirits.com.
